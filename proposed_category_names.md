# Proposed Category Names for Review

## Main Categories (5 total):

1. **Garage Doors**
   - Simple, clear, covers all door types

2. **Garage Door Openers** 
   - Covers all automatic opening systems

3. **Spare Parts & Accessories**
   - Clear that it's replacement/additional items

4. **Remote Controls**
   - Specific category for all remote devices

5. **Clearance & Special Offers**
   - Appeals to bargain hunters, clear discount section

## Subcategories (11 total):

### Under "Garage Doors":
- **New Garage Doors** - Brand new doors
- **Used & Clearance Doors** - Second-hand and discounted doors

### Under "Garage Door Openers":
- **New Openers** - Brand new opener systems
- **Used & Refurbished Openers** - Second-hand and restored openers

### Under "Spare Parts & Accessories":
- **Door Parts** - Hinges, springs, hardware for doors
- **Opener Parts** - Motors, chains, mechanical parts for openers
- **Electronics & Controls** - Circuit boards, sensors, electronic components

### Under "Remote Controls":
- **New Remote Controls** - Brand new transmitters
- **Used Remote Controls** - Second-hand remotes

### Under "Clearance & Special Offers":
- **Door Clearance** - Discounted doors
- **Parts Clearance** - Discounted parts and accessories
